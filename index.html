Welcome To The LCL!

The Learning and Cognition Lab (LCL), led by Chenglin Li, mainly focus on the research about the complex learning and cognition processes in human brain. To investegate these important research, we adopt the common methods, including behavioral experiment, EEG, fMRI, and TMS.

For more information on the topics we investigate, please refer to our research page and recent publications.
